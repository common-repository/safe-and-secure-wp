Cloud-based protection for your site.  Stop attackers from taking over your site.

== Description ==

Your WordPress site is a critical piece of your business success. Stop hackers, malicious attackers, foreign influencers from harming your site. With real-time protection and validation you’ll keep your site – and your business – safe and secure.  Sign in attempts will be logged so that you can review for peace of mind. 

== Plugin Features ==
s
Validate all sign in attempts to your WordPress site.  All attempts from non-approved countries will be blocked.  Valid countries for sign in are **United States**, **Canada**, and **United Kingdom**.  All other attempts will be blocked.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I change the list of valid sign-in countries? =

At the moment the list is not configurable but we have had a number of requests to allow this so it will be an option in a future release.
